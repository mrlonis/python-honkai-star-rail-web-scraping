Yukong Skills

Basic ATK

Arrowslinger

Single Target

Deals 50% of Yukong's ATK as Imaginary DMG to a target enemy.

Skill

Emboldening Salvo

Support

Obtains 2 stack(s) of "Roaring Bowstrings" (to a maximum of 2 stacks). When "Roaring Bowstrings" is active, the ATK of all allies increases by 40%, and every time an ally's turn ends, Yukong loses 1 stack of "Roaring Bowstrings." When it's the turn where Yukong gains "Roaring Bowstrings" by using Skill, "Roaring Bowstrings" will not be removed.

Ultimate

Diving Kestrel

Single Target

If "Roaring Bowstrings" is active on Yukong when her Ultimate is used, additionally increases all allies' CRIT Rate by 21% and CRIT DMG by 39%. At the same time, deals Imaginary DMG equal to 228% of Yukong's ATK to a single enemy.

Talent

Seven Layers, One Arrow

Enhance

Basic ATK additionally deals Imaginary DMG equal to 40% of Yukong's ATK, and increases the Toughness-Reducing DMG of this attack by 100%. This effect can be triggered again in 1 turn(s).

Technique

Chasing the Wind

Enhance

After using her Technique, Yukong enters Sprint mode for 20 seconds. In Sprint mode, her Movement SPD increases by 35%, and Yukong gains 2 stack(s) of "Roaring Bowstrings" when she enters battle by attacking enemies.

Yukong Eidolon

Eidolon 1

Aerial Marshal

At the start of battle, increases the SPD of all allies by 10% for 2 turn(s).

Eidolon 2

Skyward Command

When any ally's current energy is equal to its energy limit, Yukong regenerates an additional 5 energy. This effect can only be triggered once for each ally. The trigger count is reset after Yukong casts her Ultimate.

Eidolon 3

Torrential Fusillade

Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.

Eidolon 4

Zephyrean Echoes

When "Roaring Bowstrings" is active, Yukong deals 30% more DMG to enemies.

Eidolon 5

August Deadshot

Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Talent ATK Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.

Eidolon 6

Bowstring Thunderclap

When Yukong uses her Ultimate, she immediately gains 1 stack(s) of "Roaring Bowstrings."
