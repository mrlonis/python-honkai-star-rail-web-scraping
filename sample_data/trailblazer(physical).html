Trailblazer (Physical) Skills

Basic ATK

Farewell Hit

Single Target

Deals Physical DMG equal to 50% of the Trailblazer's ATK to a single enemy.

Skill

RIP Home Run

Blast

Deals Physical DMG equal to 62% of the Trailblazer's ATK to a single enemy and any adjacent enemies.

Ultimate

Stardust Ace

Enhance

Choose between two attack modes to deliver a full strike. Blowout: Farewell Hit deals Physical DMG equal to 300% of the Trailblazer's ATK to a single enemy. Blowout: RIP Home Run deals Physical DMG equal to 180% of the Trailblazer's ATK to a single enemy and Physical DMG equal to 108% of the Trailblazer's ATK to adjacent enemies.

Talent

Perfect Pickoff

Enhance

Each time this character inflicts Weakness Break on an enemy, ATK increases by 10%. This effect stacks up to 2 time(s).

Technique

Immortal Third Strike

Restore

Immediately heals all allies for 15% of their respective Max HP after using this Technique.

Trailblazer (Physical) Eidolon

Eidolon 1

A Falling Star

When enemies are defeated due to the Trailblazer's Ultimate, the Trailblazer regenerates 10 extra Energy. This effect can only be triggered once per attack.

Eidolon 2

An Unwilling Host

Attacking enemies with Physical Weakness restores the Trailblazer's HP equal to 5% of the Trailblazer's ATK.

Eidolon 3

A Leading Whisper

Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Talent ATK Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.

Eidolon 4

A Destructing Glance

When attacking an enemy with Weakness Break, CRIT Rate is increased by 25%.

Eidolon 5

A Surviving Hope

Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.

Eidolon 6

A Trailblazing Will

The Trailblazer's Talent is also triggered when they defeat an enemy.
