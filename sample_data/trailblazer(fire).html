Trailblazer (Fire) Skills

Basic ATK

Ice-Breaking Light

Single Target

Single Target Deals Fire DMG equal to 50% of the Trailblazer's ATK to a single enemy and gains 1 stack of Magma Will. Blast Consumes 4 stacks of Magma Will to enhance Basic ATK, dealing Fire DMG equal to 90% of the Trailblazer's ATK to a single enemy and Fire DMG to equal to 36% of the Trailblazer's ATK to enemies adjacent to it.

Skill

Ever-Burning Amber

Defense

Increases the Trailblazer's DMG Reduction by 40% and gains 1 stack of Magma Will, with a 100% base chance to Taunt all enemies for 1 turn(s).

Ultimate

War-Flaming Lance

AoE ATK

Deals Fire DMG equal to 50% of the Trailblazer's ATK plus 75% of the Trailblazer's DEF to all enemies. The next Basic ATK will be automatically enhanced and does not cost Magma Will.

Talent

Treasure of the Architects

Enhance

Each time the Trailblazer is hit, they gain 1 stack of Magma Will for a max of 8 stack(s). When Magma Will has no fewer than 4 stacks, the Trailblazer's Basic ATK becomes enhanced, dealing DMG to a single enemy and enemies adjacent to it. When the Trailblazer uses Basic ATK, Skill, or Ultimate, apply a Shield to all allies that absorbs DMG equal to 4% of the Trailblazer's DEF plus 20. The Shield lasts for 2 turn(s).

Technique

Call of the Guardian

Defense

After using Technique, at the start of the next battle, gains a Shield that absorbs DMG equal to 30% of the Trailblazer's DEF plus 384 for 1 turn(s).

Trailblazer (Fire) Eidolon

Eidolon 1

Earth-Shaking Resonance

When the Trailblazer uses their Basic ATK, additionally deals Fire DMG equal to 25% of the Trailblazer's DEF. When the Trailblazer uses their enhanced Basic ATK, additionally deals Fire DMG equal to 50% of the Trailblazer's DEF.

Eidolon 2

Time-Defying Tenacity

The Shield applied to all allies from the Trailblazer's Talent will block extra DMG equal to 2% of the Trailblazer's DEF plus 27.

Eidolon 3

Trail-Blazing Blueprint

Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Talent ATK Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.

Eidolon 4

Nation-Building Oath

At the start of the battle, immediately gains 4 stack(s) of Magma Will.

Eidolon 5

Spirit-Warming Flame

Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.

Eidolon 6

City-Forging Bulwarks

After the Trailblazer use enhanced Basic ATK or Ultimate, their DEF increases by 10%. Stacks up to 3 time(s).
