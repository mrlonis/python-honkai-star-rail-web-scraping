March 7th Skills

Basic ATK

Frigid Cold Arrow

Single Target

Deals Ice DMG equal to 50% of March 7th's ATK to a single enemy.

Skill

The Power of Cuteness

Defense

Provides a single ally with a Shield that can absorb DMG equal to 38% of March 7th's DEF plus 190 for 3 turn(s). If the ally's HP is 30% of their Max HP or higher, greatly increases the chance of enemies attacking that ally.

Ultimate

Glacial Cascade

AoE ATK

Deals Ice DMG equal to 90% of March 7th's ATK to all enemies. Hit enemies have a 50% base chance to be Frozen for 1 turn(s). While Frozen, the enemy cannot take action and will receive Additional Ice DMG equal to 30% of March 7th's ATK at the beginning of each turn.

Talent

Girl Power

Single Target

After a shielded ally is attacked by an enemy, March 7th immediately Counters, dealing Ice DMG equal to 50% of her ATK. This effect can be triggered 2 time(s) each turn.

Technique

Freezing Beauty

Immediately attacks the enemy. After entering battle, there is a 100% base chance to Freeze a random enemy for 1 turn(s). While Frozen, the enemy cannot move and will take Ice DMG equal to 50% of March 7th's ATK at the beginning of each turn.

March 7th Eidolon

Eidolon 1

Memory of You

Every time March 7th's Ultimate Freeze a target, she regenerates 6 Energy.

Eidolon 2

Memory of It

Upon entering battle, grants a Shield equal to 24% of March 7th's DEF plus 320 to the ally with the lowest HP percentage, lasting for 3 turn(s).

Eidolon 3

Memory of Everything

Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.

Eidolon 4

Never Forfeit Again

The Talent's Counter effect can be triggered 1 more time in each turn. The DMG dealt by Counter increases by an amount that is equal to 30% of March 7th's DEF.

Eidolon 5

Never Forget Again

Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Talent ATK Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.

Eidolon 6

Just Like This, Always...

Allies under the protection of the Shield granted by the Skill restores HP equal to 4% of their Max HP plus 106 at the beginning of each turn.
