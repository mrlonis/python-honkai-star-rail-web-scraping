Natasha Skills

Basic ATK

Behind the Kindness

Single Target

Deals Physical DMG equal to 50% of Natasha's ATK to a single enemy.

Skill

Love, Heal, and Choose

Restore

Restores a single ally for 7% of Natasha's Max HP plus 70. Restores the ally for another 4.8% of Natasha's Max HP plus 48 at the beginning of each turn for 2 turn(s).

Ultimate

Gift of Rebirth

Restore

Heals all allies for 9.2% of Natasha's Max HP plus 92.

Talent

Innervation

Restore

When healing allies with HP percentage at 30% or lower, increases Natasha's Outgoing Healing by 25%. This effect also works on continuous healing.

Technique

Hypnosis Research

Immediately attacks the enemy. After entering battle, deals Physical DMG equal to 80% of Natasha's ATK to a random enemy, with a 100% base chance to Weaken all enemies. While Weakened, enemies deal 30% less DMG to allies for 1 turn(s).

Natasha Eidolon

Eidolon 1

Phamacology Expertise

After being attacked, if the current HP percentage is 30% or lower, heals self for 1 time to restore HP by an amount equal to 15% of Max HP plus 400. This effect can only be triggered 1 time per battle.

Eidolon 2

Clinical Research

When Natasha uses her Ultimate, grant continuous healing for 1 turn(s) to allies whose HP is at 30% or lower. And at the beginning of their turn, their HP is restored by an amount equal to 6% of Natasha's Max HP plus 160.

Eidolon 3

The Right Cure

Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.

Eidolon 4

Miracle Cure

After being attacked, regenerates 5 extra Energy.

Eidolon 5

Preventive Treatment

Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Talent Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.

Eidolon 6

Doctor's Grace

Natasha's Basic ATK additionally deals Physical DMG equal to 40% of her Max HP.
